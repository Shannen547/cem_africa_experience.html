My Experience at the CEM Africa Awards 2024

Attending the CEM Africa Awards conference in 2024 was an enriching experience that offered invaluable insights into customer experience (CX) and employee experience (EX). Over the two-day event, I participated in several workshops that provided practical knowledge and innovative strategies to enhance both CX and EX. Here’s a detailed account of my experience at each workshop, along with some key takeaways.

Day 1: Tuesday, 30 July 2024

Workshop Session 1: AI’s Impact on the Customer and Employee Experience

Speaker: Tevin Tawamba, Solution Consultant, Genesys

Key Takeaways:

  • Practical Applications of AI in Enhancing CX and EX: Genesys uses AI to personalize experiences across multiple channels like chat, email, messaging, voice, social, and text. Their AI solutions help optimize customer interactions and improve employee productivity through intelligent automation and embedded AI tools.
  • AI as a Catalyst for Better Customer and Employee Experiences: AI simplifies management tasks, drives employee engagement, and enhances customer loyalty. Integrating AI enables businesses to create empathetic and personalized interactions, leading to improved customer satisfaction and employee retention.
  • Demonstrations of AI-Powered Solutions: Real-world examples and case studies, such as The National Domestic Violence Hotline using AI-powered tools to support survivors more efficiently, highlight the effectiveness of AI solutions in real-life scenarios.

Workshop Session 2: How to Use Proactive Conversational AI to Supercharge Existing Outbound Approaches in Financial Services

Speaker: Edward De Mas Latrie, Regional Sales Director, ContactEngine a NICE Company

Key Takeaways:

  • Benefits of Proactive Conversational AI: NICE leverages AI to anticipate customer needs and engage proactively, resulting in improved customer satisfaction and loyalty. This approach creates more personalized and timely interactions.
  • Enhancing Outbound Strategies in Financial Services: NICE’s AI solutions enable financial services to enhance their outbound strategies by providing targeted, timely, and relevant communications, boosting customer engagement and conversion rates.
  • Reducing Costs and Increasing Revenue through AI: NICE uses AI to optimize operations, reduce manual tasks, and enhance decision-making, leading to cost savings and increased revenue by improving efficiency and effectiveness in customer interactions.

Workshop Session 3: Securing Data-Driven Customer Insights & Personalization

Speakers:

  • Rebone Gcabo, PhD, Head, Taxpayer and Trader Education Products (TTEP), National Operations Scheduling and Enablement (NOSE)
  • Marianne Kekana, Chief Data and Analytics Officer, ConnectMe, FNB South Africa
  • Gys Kappers, CEO, DataProphet
  • Dr. Jeanne Fredericks, Founder and CEO, The Change Hub
  • Grant Hughes, IT Manager, Cybersecurity & GRC, Engen

Key Takeaways from Rebone Gcabo:

  • Importance of Securing Customer Data: SARS protects taxpayer information through secure digital channels, stringent data protection policies, and regular audits to ensure compliance and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Techniques for Data-Driven Personalization: SARS uses advanced data analytics to personalize taxpayer interactions and services, improving the efficiency and accuracy of tax-related processes.
  • Strategies for Effective Change Management: SARS implements structured change management strategies to adapt to new regulations and technological advancements, ensuring smooth transitions and minimal disruption to taxpayer services.

Key Takeaways from Marianne Kekana:

  • Importance of Securing Customer Data: FNB prioritizes data security through robust encryption, secure login procedures, and continuous monitoring to protect customer information from unauthorized access and cyber threats.
  • Techniques for Data-Driven Personalization: FNB utilizes advanced data analytics to tailor financial products and services to individual customer needs, enhancing user experience through personalized recommendations and targeted offers.
  • Strategies for Effective Change Management: FNB implements comprehensive change management strategies, including staff training, customer communication, and phased implementation to ensure smooth transitions and minimize disruptions.
